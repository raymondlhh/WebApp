Rewards FAQ

What are reward points?

Reward points are loyalty points you earn by making purchases or participating in promotions. They can be redeemed for special rewards.

How do I earn points?

You earn points by making purchases, referring friends, or participating in special events.

How can I use my points?

You can redeem your points for rewards listed on the Rewards page, subject to availability and terms.

Do points expire?

Points may expire after a certain period. Please check the validity of your points in your account.

Who can I contact for questions about points?

If you have questions, please contact our support team via the Contacts page.
